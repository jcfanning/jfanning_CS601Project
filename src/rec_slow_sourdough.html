Slow Sourdough

Description:

This recipe if for a fairly simple sourdough loaf. There is a long chilled proof involved so it will take >12 hours to complete. This particular recipe utilizes a cloche in order to provide a controlled steam environment for the bread. Adaptions will have to be made if you do not have one. One possibility is to use a pizza stone and a thick pan that boiling water can be poured into when the bread is placed in the oven. Overall cook time will likely be effected. Look for the internal bread temp to be about 210F.

Ingredients:

Ingredient Weight Bakers's Percent
Bread Flour 412.61g 100%
Water 284.24g 68.9%
Salt 11.46g 2.78%
Starter 91.69g 22.22%
Malt .83g .2%

Instructions:

The Dough:

  1. Combine the flour and malt in a bowl, or stand mixer bowl
  2. Wisk salt into flour to disperse evenly
  3. Combine the water and the starter in another bowl
  4. Whisk starter into water
  5. Create a well in the middle of the flour, and pour the water starter mixture into it
  6. Mix, by hand or with mixer, untill flour and water are incorperated
  7. Sprinkle top surface with half of the salt, turn over and sprinkle bottom with other half.
  8. Cover and ley autolyze for 30 minutes
  9. Knead the dough untill silky
  10. Let rise for 1.5 hrs on the counter.
  11. Fold with the lift and lay method
  12. Let rise for 1.5 hours hours on the counter.
  13. Preshape the dough and let it resut under a damp towell for 10 minutes
  14. Shape the dough and place into baskets once the scar seals up.
  15. Let the Dough proof for 1.5 hours
  16. Place in fridge for min 12 hours.

Baking:

  1. Preheat oven to 470F
  2. Once at temperature, place cloche in the oven to preheat for 30 minutes
  3. At the same time remove the chilled load from the fridge
  4. After waiting the 30 minutes, remove the cloche bottom from the oven
  5. Turn the loaf out the cloche bottom
  6. Score loaf with a razor blade or lame
  7. Place into the oven and cover
  8. Reduce oven temp to 435F
  9. Bake for 37 minutes
  10. Then remove the lid, and bake for 10 minutes more
  11. Bread should be done and read to be place on a rack to cool
