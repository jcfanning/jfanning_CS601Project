A Simple Pizza Dough Recipe

Description:

A sourdough pizza dough recipe. This recipe was developed from one that used yeast instead of a starter. If you don't have a starter on hand or need to make the dough quicker, yeast could be used again. To do so, add 59 grams of water and flour to the recipe. As well as about 18g of yeast.

Ingredients:

Ingredient Weight Bakers's Percent
AP Flour 530.87g 100%
Water 383.41g 72.22%
Salt 14.75g 2.78%
Starter 117.97g 22.22%

Instructions:

  1. Combine the flour and salt in a bowl, or stand mixer bowl
  2. Wisk salt into flour to disperse evenly
  3. Combine the water and the starter in another bowl
  4. Whisk starter into water
  5. Create a well in the middle of the flour, and pour the water starter mixture into it
  6. Mix, by hand or with mixer, untill flour and water are incorperated
  7. Cover and let autolyze for 30 minutes
  8. Knead dough by hand or with mixer until silky in texture
  9. Let rise for 1.5 hours
  10. Place in the fridge for 12 hours
  11. Devide dough into three equal portions
  12. Use or freeze until needed
